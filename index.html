Projects I've Worked On

100 Days of UI

The 100 Days of UI challenge is a design challenge where participants take on a User Interface design prompt for 100 days. I am currently in the progress of undertaking this challenge to further my visual design skills and to challenge myself at creating better design solutions.

CogSci Web Contest Online Banners

The UCSD Department of Cognitive Science held a contest for the purpose of revamping the department website. Submissions needed to include varying elements of the six specializations the Cogntive Science Major had to offer: Language and Culture, Design and Interaction, Neuroscience, Machine Learning, Clinical Aspects, and Cognitive Behavioral Neuroscience.

For this project, I wanted to create a minimalistic design that incorporated the necessary graphic and artistic elements that fulfilled the submission requirements. I looked at different department websites as references and guides. I intended for each specialization to have a banner of it's own so I wanted to use a design that was replicable and flexible. I had the honor of being one of two winners for this contest.
