about

Hello! I'm Eunice and I'm currently a third year Cognitive Science Major with a specialization in Design and Interaction at UC San Diego. I was born and raised in the Bay Area where I was surrounded by my three dogs, good Taiwanese food, and plenty of boba.


I never really knew what I wanted to be when I grew up but I liked helping people and I liked creating things. Design allows me to bridge both of my passions into one field, where I can communicate with users to create something that serves their needs. Whether it's developing a virtual reality application for a student organization or creating a productivity application to help sick college students with their studies, I enjoy being able to experience design's versatility and gaining new pearls of wisdom along the way.
