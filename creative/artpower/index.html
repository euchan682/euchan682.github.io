ARTPOWER

GRAPHIC DESIGN | MARKETING | STRATEGY

ArtPower at UC San Diego aims to energize, engage, and transform campus cultural life.


As an Arts Org Intern, I'm responsible for designing marketing collateral for print and media as well as promoting the arts through social media and community outreach. It is also my responsibility to develop marketing strategies that increase student participation whether it was through promotion of free tickets or maintaining relationships with student organizations.


Whether it's curating content for our Facebook page, or making cool spreads on our Instagram, being a part of ArtPower is a great place for creatives to grow and flourish. As an ArtPower representative, I advertise to a diverse community and witness firsthand how an appreciation for the arts transcends all ages and cultures.
