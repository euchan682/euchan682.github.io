PHOTOGRAPHY

DESIGN | MEDIA

In my senior year of high school, I was a layout designer for the school yearbook. Part of my responsibility was also to go to all school events and take photos. Whether it was prom, water polo matches, or the annual school cardboard box boat race, I had a lot of fun capturing candid moments of my friends and classmates. Entering college, I used my first paycheck to buy my own camera. Since then, I've taken it everywhere.


I love being able to tell stories, and I found that photography is one way to do so. Whether it's travel, landscape, or portraiture photography, preserving special moments has always been one of my many small joys.
